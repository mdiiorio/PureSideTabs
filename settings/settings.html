Pure Side Tabs Settings

Controls how many recently visited tabs appear in the popup menu.

When enabled, opening a new tab while viewing a tab contained by a tab group causes the new tab to open at the end of that group instead of at the end of the window.

When enabled, tab groups always appear at the top (after pinned tabs). New tabs opened before any group are automatically moved after all groups, and dragging a tab above the groups section is disallowed.
